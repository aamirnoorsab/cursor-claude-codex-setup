\## Post 1 — 1 Billion Data Points Across 14 Studies: What We Learned About AI Search Optimization



Ahrefs' large-scale research (1B+ data points, 14 studies) on AI search optimization. The single most data-dense source in this collection.



Key findings:

1\. "Best X" listicles = 43.8% of all page types cited by ChatGPT (most-cited content format).

2\. 67% of ChatGPT's top 1,000 citations come from sources marketers can't influence (Wikipedia 29.7%, homepages 23.8%, app stores 6.6%); only 32.3% is influenceable content.

3\. 28.3% of ChatGPT's most-cited pages have zero Google organic visibility — a separate discovery layer.

4\. ChatGPT cites only \~50% of URLs it retrieves; being retrieved ≠ being cited.

5\. Schema markup had zero meaningful impact on AI citations (myth-buster).

6\. YouTube mentions have the highest correlation (0.737) with AI brand visibility of all factors studied — higher than backlinks, DR, page count.

7\. AI Overviews reduce clicks to the #1 result by 58% (up from 34.5% ten months earlier).

8\. 99.9% of AI Overviews appear on informational queries; shopping triggers AIOs only 3.2% of the time.

9\. AI Mode and AI Overviews reach the same conclusion 86% of the time but cite almost entirely different sources (13.7% overlap).

10\. AI Overviews change every 2.15 days on average (70% of content differs between observations) but semantic similarity stays \~0.95 — words/sources shuffle, meaning barely moves.

\## Post 2 — Google I/O Takeaway: From Counting Clicks to Tracking Brand Mentions



Soulo's analysis of Google I/O's AI Search implications for marketers. Reports Google losing \~5% of traffic share in 10 months (35.11% → 30.53% across \~75k sites in Ahrefs' panel). Core argument: the game is shifting from counting clicks to tracking how often AI mentions your brand/products — brand visibility inside AI answers has direct business value.



Note: post closes with an Ahrefs Brand Radar product mention. The analysis (traffic-share decline, clicks-to-mentions shift) is the substantive part; the closing is promotional.
